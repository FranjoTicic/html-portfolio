The best 5 Anime to watch according to Franjo

This is the list of best Anime I've watched recently

One Piece

One of best world buildings, character development and story-line itself. 10/10 would recommend.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Amazing battle animations, very likeable characters, great world design.

Ragna Crimson

Fresh anime that aired two months ago, amazing story-line with amazing character development,
Anime itself kind of goes to isekai edition, but the main character has yet to unlock his godlike potential.

The time I reincarnated as a slime

Isekai anime with loveable slime main character that does his own world building and does a wonderful job making his kingdom along with various friendships.

Eminence in the shadow

I just love this isekai because I love anime bewbs with juicy nice tiddies and big, well shaped, phat bundas.
